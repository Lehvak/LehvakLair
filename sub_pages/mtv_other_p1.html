What is the MTV label

Manufacturing test version for a specific batch or iteration of BOM (Build of Materials), the hardware product Identification label. Located by the fans on most PCBs, or towards the front of the PCB behind the ROL in the designated silkscreen location.
It will give:

  • The internal part number that references the BOM and revision of BOM (if applicable).
  • MTV specific formatted serial
  • The name of the BOM, or ConfigID (if applicable)
  • Unit production number (if applicable)
